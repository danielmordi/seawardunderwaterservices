About us

Seaward underwater services is a leading commercial diving company operating in the maritime industry with a highly skilled technician base of professional divers that are full time employed in 13 locations across 6 countries and numerous working partnerships worldwide. Seaward underwater services has an unparalleled technical capability to deliver turn-key solutions at a global scale.

We continually strive on developing new underwater technologies and advanced service/repair methods focused on efficiencies and safety to our customers, to be “the one source for global underwater solutions”.
